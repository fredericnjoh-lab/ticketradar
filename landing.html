FAQ

Common questions

What markets and platforms do you cover?
We scan SeatGeek and Ticketmaster live, plus any events you add via Google Sheets. Coverage spans US, UK, and EU markets across concerts, F1, UFC/MMA, football, and festivals. Resale margin calculations factor in 15% platform fees (StubHub, Viagogo, etc.).
How accurate are the margin calculations?
Margins are calculated in real time using live asking prices from SeatGeek and Ticketmaster, net of a 15% seller fee. Face values come from official listings or your Google Sheet. The AI layer adds artist popularity data from Last.fm to flag high-demand events that historically sell above average.
Can I track my own inventory and P&L?
Yes. The Kanban board lets you move tickets through Watch, Bought, Selling, and Sold columns. It calculates latent profit, realized gains, and total portfolio value automatically. You can also add custom events with quantity, purchase price, and notes.
Is my Telegram token safe?
Your Telegram bot token never touches the frontend. All notifications are routed through the backend server. The token is stored as an environment variable on Render and is never exposed in client-side code or API responses.
